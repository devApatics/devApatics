Study Showed That 10% Of Workers Comp
Claims Paid Were Fraudulent

A 2021 APATICS analysis of over four (4) million workers compensation claims paid showed that 19,612 providers out of a network of 86,000 submitted fraudulent claims totaling about 10% of all claims paid during 2020. This study was conducted retrospectively using 2020 claims and network provider data for a regional workers compensation payer that paid about $107 million in fraudulent claims on approximately $1.17 billion in total payments.
